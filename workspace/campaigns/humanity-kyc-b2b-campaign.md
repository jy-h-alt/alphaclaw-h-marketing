# Humanity KYC — B2B Campaign Plan

**Product:** Humanity KYC — Interoperable KYC with Humanity SDK
**Tagline:** One KYC, reusable everywhere.
**Target:** PayFi, onramp/offramp, RWA tokenization, exchanges, crypto projects needing KYC
**Goals:** Lead generation, brand awareness, product launch
**Channels:** Content marketing, events, Twitter/X ads

---

## 1. Positioning & Messaging

### Core Value Prop
Every crypto project rebuilds KYC from scratch — fragmented, expensive, and a friction point that kills conversion. Humanity KYC lets users verify once and carry that verification everywhere via the Humanity SDK.

### Key Messages by Audience

| Audience | Pain Point | Message |
|---|---|---|
| **Exchanges** | Regulatory pressure + user drop-off at KYC | "Cut onboarding friction by 70%. Users arrive pre-verified." |
| **PayFi / Onramp-Offramp** | Compliance cost per user is high | "Stop paying for KYC your users already completed somewhere else." |
| **RWA Platforms** | Need institutional-grade compliance | "Interoperable identity that satisfies regulators across jurisdictions." |
| **DeFi / dApps** | Want compliance without centralization | "SDK-native KYC. Decentralized ethos, compliant reality." |

### Differentiators to Hammer
- **Interoperable** — one verification, every platform
- **SDK-first** — devs integrate in hours, not weeks
- **User-owned** — identity travels with the user, not siloed per platform
- **Cost reduction** — platforms share verification costs instead of duplicating them

## 3. Twitter/X Ads Strategy

### Campaign Structure

**Campaign 1: Awareness (Weeks 1–3)**
- Objective: Impressions + engagement
- Targeting: Crypto founders, CTO/CPOs, compliance officers, web3 devs
- Creative: Problem-awareness content ("Your users hate doing KYC 5 times")
- Budget split: 40%

**Campaign 2: Consideration (Weeks 2–6)**
- Objective: Website clicks + SDK page visits
- Targeting: Retarget engagers from Campaign 1 + lookalikes
- Creative: Solution-focused ("One integration. Every user pre-verified.")
- Budget split: 35%

**Campaign 3: Conversion (Weeks 4–8)**
- Objective: Demo requests / SDK signups
- Targeting: Warm audiences, retargeting site visitors
- Creative: Direct CTA ("Book a demo" / "Start integrating today")
- Budget split: 25%

### Targeting Layers
- **Interest:** Cryptocurrency, blockchain development, fintech, DeFi
- **Follower lookalikes:** @0xPolygon, @Chainalysis, @Sumsub, @Onfido, @circle, @MoonPay
- **Keywords:** KYC, compliance, onramp, identity verification, web3 compliance
- **Geo:** Global, with emphasis on crypto-friendly jurisdictions (UAE, Singapore, EU, US)

### Ad Formats
- Single image with bold stat ("5x KYC = 5x user drop-off")
- Short video (15–30s explainer)
- Thread promotion (organic threads that perform well → boost)
